About Brush and Bramble

Brush and Bramble is a small business that started in 2015. As a college student, I was looking for a way to make some extra money, so I took something that I love and am good at and starting selling them to friends and family and also giving them to others as gifts. What started out as a very small local business, is now a growing business in the OC area. I love taking the gifts that I have been given and using them to bless, encourage, and uplift others through simple things such as home and wedding decor.
